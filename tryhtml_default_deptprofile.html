Procurement Department Introduction

Purchasing

  • Ensure that purchasing process and systems are in place to receive user requisitions and proper authorization of the spend
  • Create the purchase order and sending it to the vendor
  • Escalate the orders to ensure timely delivery of the product and services required to operate the business
  • Price negotiation

Strategic Sourcing

  • Set up strategic sourcing process to ensure that all suppliers on-boarding follow a standard process
  • Run the RFI (Request for information) or RFP (Request for Proposal) process with the suppliers Flows
  • Support stakeholder team with analysis of the RFP responses and help drive a decision which meets stakeholder team goals (Scorecard)
  • Own a savings target and responsible for tracking procurement cost savings

Contract Management

  • Review supplier contracts to ensure that they meet the defined company terms
  • Frontend the legal terms negotiation with the suppliers
  • Provide standard contract templates for different product and services which are procured by CoStar
  • Execute contract

Data Analytics and Reporting

  • Run standard spend visibility reports
  • Conduct spend analysis on saving opportunities
  • Advise CostCenter owner on budget and spending Spending real time

Stakeholders Support and Trainings

  • Educate stakeholders about the purchasing process
  • Play as centralized procurement support center
  • Regularly standard of procedure revise
